Smoke-Free Together

Your guide to smoking cessation during pregnancy with the support of a loved one. 

Begin your smoke-free journey!

Smoking cessation during pregnancy starts together!

Start on your way together with someone dear who would join you. We‘re bringing you a mobile app to guide you on the smoke-free journey. Together we’re teaming up for the health of the baby!

The mobile app contains information on the effects of smoking during pregnancy, practical advice, instruments for planning to quit, activities that you can do together and a function that allows communication between the pregnant smoker’s app and that of the peer supporter. 

How does the program work?

After enrollment, together with your peer supporter, you will be randomly allocated into one of the participant groups. This could be:

(1) The one in which you use the Smoke-Free Together mobile app, and you will benefit from up to 10 specialized counseling sessions, according to your needs.

(2) The one in which you will receive an informative brochure on the benefits of smoking cessation.

What happens afterwards?

At 1 and at 3 months after birth, we will contact you to fill in a follow-up survey (15-20 minutes).

At 6 months after birth, we will contact you and your peer supporter for the final survey.
