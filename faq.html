Smoke-Free Together

What does the app offer?

The mobile app contains information on the effects of smoking during pregnancy, practical advice, instruments for planning to quit, activities that you can do together and a function that allows communication between the pregnant smoker’s app and that of the peer supporter. 

Scientific information

on benefits and methods for quitting, including a ‘Panic’ section for the most difficult moments.

Personalized content

according to pregnancy week, to how you feel and your personal barriers in cessation.

Support from someone close to you

The app is connected with that of the peer supporter, who learns how to communicate and help the future mother 

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can be my peer supporter?

Anyone close to you, if they are over 18 years old, speak Romanian and have an Android operating system smartphone with internet access. Our advise is that you choose someone who you know would dedicate their time to you, and to whom you can speak comfortably about the hardships of quitting smoking.

How will my privacy be protected?

We assure you that you will not be judged for any of the information or opinions you express during participation. Also, only the members of the program team will have access to the information you provided and to documents used during the research program. 

Will I be rewarded financially for participating?

You and your peer supporter will receive 5 online gift cards for the amount of 50 RON each (~10USD), as follows: one each after enrolment, and after completing participation, for you as well as for your peer supporter, and another for you, after completion of the 3-month follow-up survey, as a thank-you for your time.

Can I use the app if I do not enrol in the program?

At the moment, no. This project aims to demonstrate that the app helps in quitting smoking. That is why we are grateful to all participants, who help us obtain the data that could lead to the app being used at a larger scale.
